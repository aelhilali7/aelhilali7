## 🚀 What I Do

- 🔐 **Secure by Design** — I architect hardened, identity-first Kubernetes platforms with Zero-Trust principles from day one.
- ☸️ **Kubernetes at Scale** — Multi-cluster, HA setups on AKS & on-prem, hardened with Network Policies, Cilium/eBPF, OPA, and RBAC.
